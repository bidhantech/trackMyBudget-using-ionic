About
trackMyBudget is an app which will help you track your earnings & expenses. With the help of this app, you will be able analyze your spending habits & control unnecessary spending.

Some of it's core features:
Easy to use: Easily add earnings & expenses and see previous entries.
Slide to delete: Made a wrong entry? slide left on the item to delete it.
Works offline: Privacy first, all you data is saved locally in the app.

Upcoming features:
Charts: You'll be able to easily visualize your spending habits using easy to understand graphs.
